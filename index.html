Why the Pacific Northwest

Cascadia, mostly known as the Pacfifc Northwest lies between the Pacific Ocean on the West and the loose boundary of the Cascade Range to the East. The PNW comprises of active volcanoes, coastal forrests, beaches and mountains in both the US and British Columbia, passing the US border.

The Pacific Northwest

Learn about different parks within the PNW.

Denali

State: Alaska
Peak Season: May-Mid September
Area in Square Miles: 9492
Also see: Wonder Lake, Mount Foraker, Kahiltna Glacier, Ruth Glacier, Mount Hunter, Muldrow Glacier, Horseshoe Lake, Savage Lake, Teklanika River, Thorofare Pass and Mount McKinley.
